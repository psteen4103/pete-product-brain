## Strategic Pillars

These three pillars guide prioritization decisions and resource allocation. Every major initiative should map to at least one pillar.

### 1. Frictionless Experience
*Remove every obstacle between intent and adventure.*

- **Why it matters:** 68% cart abandonment means customers want our gear but can't get through checkout. Mobile conversion lags desktop by 14 points. Friction costs us $20M+ annually.
- **2026 Focus:** [Checkout Redesign](/specs/checkout-redesign.md), [Mobile App v3](/specs/mobile-app-v3.md)
- **Success Metric:** Checkout conversion from 32% â†’ 45%

### 2. Earned Loyalty
*Turn transactions into relationships through genuine value exchange.*

- **Why it matters:** Members spend 2.3x more than guests, but our loyalty NPS (+12) trails our brand NPS (+45) by 33 points. The program isn't earning its keep.
- **2026 Focus:** [Trail Rewards v2](/specs/loyalty-program-v2.md), [Returns Experience](/specs/returns-experience.md)
- **Success Metric:** Loyalty program NPS from +12 â†’ +35

### 3. Sustainability Leadership
*Make Contoso the default choice for eco-conscious adventurers.*

- **Why it matters:** 67% of customers say sustainability influences their purchase decisions, but only 23% know about our eco initiatives. We're leading without getting credit.
- **2026 Focus:** [Sustainability Dashboard](/specs/sustainability-dashboard.md), eco-product discovery
- **Success Metric:** Sustainability awareness from 23% â†’ 60%
